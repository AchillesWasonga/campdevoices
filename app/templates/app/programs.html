Our Programs

Learn about the various programs we offer at Camp de Voices.

Tuko Freshy Dhuleni

This program focuses on providing outdoor activities that enhance teamwork, critical thinking, and personal growth. Participants engage in hiking, camping, and community service projects.

Tuko Freshy Mtaani

Tuko Freshy Mtaani is an urban outreach program designed to empower young individuals through workshops and mentorship. Participants connect with professionals from diverse fields and gain skills for community-based projects.

Changamkia Climate

Changamkia Climate aims to educate participants on climate change and environmental conservation. Through workshops and hands-on projects, we encourage young people to take meaningful action for a sustainable future.

Leadership Exchange Experience

The Leadership Exchange Experience is a unique program for young leaders to learn from each other. Participants engage in leadership seminars, networking sessions, and exchange experiences that broaden their perspectives.

Training Trainers

Training Trainers focuses on equipping participants with the skills to become effective facilitators and trainers. This program provides them with the tools and techniques necessary for leading community workshops and training sessions.
